About Me

My name is Hovannes, I am 21 years old. I study at the Gyumri branch of the European University of Armenia in the department of "Jurisprudence". Since childhood, I have loved mathematics and the latest technologies. I took a number of programming courses, thanks to which I gained a lot of knowledge in this area. I also participated in a number of informal educational programs, gained knowledge from various fields. In 2020, I went to compulsory military service and participated in the Artsakh War. I was discharged in 2022. After demobilization, I decided not to be content with university knowledge and simultaneously engage in programming. I like to be self-educated. I know MS Office and I can work with git-github/gitlab. You can see my work in the "Work" section.
